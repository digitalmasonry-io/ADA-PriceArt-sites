Help

Welcome to PriceArt! Here is an explanation of the interactivity and some tips to get started.

  • For the best experience, use a desktop computer on chrome.
  • If it is not rendering properly, refresh the page or try switching browsers.
  • To interact with your NFT, click and drag on the canvas and play with the buttons in the settings bar.
    • pause pauses and plays the animation.
    • zoom_in magnifies the Cardano logo.
    • view_in_ar turns your candle into a skeleton outline.
    • wallpaper turns on animation trailing.
